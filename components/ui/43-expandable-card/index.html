Start with a mix of "browns" — dry leaves, cardboard, straw — and "greens" like vegetable peels and coffee grounds. Keep the pile roughly three parts brown to one part green, turning it every week or two to bring in air.

  • Layer materials rather than dumping all at once
  • Keep moisture like a wrung-out sponge
  • Chop larger scraps to speed decomposition
  • Finished compost smells earthy, not sour
