Soil

Good soil is alive — full of worms, fungi, and last year's compost breaking down into next year's harvest.

Planting

Sow seeds at twice their depth, water gently, and give seedlings room to stretch their roots.

Watering

Deep and infrequent beats shallow and daily. Roots follow water down when it's given generously.

Harvest

Pick in the cool of morning. What you gather today feeds the table tonight.
