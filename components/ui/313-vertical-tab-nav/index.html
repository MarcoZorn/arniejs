Spring

Cool soil warms slowly. Sow peas and greens early, and hold tender seedlings back until the last frost has passed.

Summer

Growth peaks fast. Water deeply in the early morning and keep an eye out for pests drawn to the heat.

Autumn

Harvest before the first hard frost, and use the cooling weeks to sow cover crops that protect the soil.

Winter

Little grows, but plenty can be planned. Mend tools, rotate beds on paper, and let the ground rest.
