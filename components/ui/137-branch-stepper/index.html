Plant Your Garden

Follow the branch to grow your first plot, one step at a time.

Step 1 of 5

Choose a Plot

Pick a sun-facing patch of earth. Good drainage and morning light will give your seedlings the strongest start.

Step 2 of 5

Prepare the Soil

Turn the earth, clear the stones, and work in a little compost. Rich, loose soil is the root of everything that follows.

Step 3 of 5

Sow the Seeds

Space your seeds evenly and press them gently into the soil. Water well and let patience do the rest.

Step 4 of 5

Tend & Grow

Water on a steady rhythm, pull the weeds, and watch the first true leaves unfurl toward the sun.

Step 5 of 5

Harvest

Gather what you've grown. Every ripe fruit is a small reward for the branch you've climbed to get here.
